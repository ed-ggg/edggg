< Water-Based Life >

Green Gas Giants with Water-Based Life are the most infamous amongst the known GGG classes for many reasons. The majority of them share a dark red base cloud layer, which is quite starkly contrasted by the usually massive bands and streaks of green clouds. Other colors that can be found on them are bright pink, a dark tan color, and an even darker red, almost a black color. Note that due to a codex bug, this planet class is unable to be reported to the codex. Its 'green' codex slot is instead erroneously taken by Green Gas Giants with Ammonia-Based Life.

"This particular gas giant exhibits vivid colouration due to the presence of free-floating radioplankton — carbon-based algae that extract energy from the planet's intense radiation flux." - System map description
