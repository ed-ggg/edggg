Ammonia-Based Life

Green Gas Giants with Ammonia-Based Life are an interesting bunch, with each being visually distinct in their own right. All of them share the normal dirty pale-green base color, as well as an either dark red, or dark purple-red secondary color to go along with green. A few show distinct banding, but the majority have their discolored clouds sprinkled about the surface layer, in spotty patches or thin streaks. Some also show significant polar discoloration. This class' codex entry is erroneously entered under the 'Green Gas Giant with Water-Based Life' section, preventing the Green 'Water-Based Life' Gas giants from having an entry.
