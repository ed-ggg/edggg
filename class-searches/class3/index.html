Class III

Class III Green Gas Giants are famously known for their wildly different surface cloud colors. These planets can range from barely any green at all, to being fully enveloped in blinding green clouds. These planets can be discolored to have normal blue, deep purple, bright and deep pink, and of course green. This planet class was discovered to be green at only certain temperatures (save for 2).
