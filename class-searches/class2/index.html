Class II

Class II Green Gas Giants share a similar color scheme between them, keeping their usual chalky-white base color. What would be lighter golden regions on normal Class II gas giants is replaced with a bright pink color, and the dark brown colors, usually consisting of and surrounding storms, are replaced with bright green. Because of the nature of all Class II gas giant cloud patterns, this class shows the least overall color variation compared to their standard counterparts.

"This particular gas giant sustains free-floating radioplankton — carbon-based algae that extract energy from the planet's intense radiation flux." - System map description
